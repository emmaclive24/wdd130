Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this plan and rafting site is to have an online presence for our customers. This will allow us to be more organized and flexible with scheduling. It will also relieve presure from our staff of doing this manually or over the phone. This will also provide a better showcase of the different trips and packages to our customers. This will help us bring in more customer and build revenue We will be able to share information with our customer We will be able to recieve feedback and opinions from our clients We can also connect with our customers by sharing insights.

Audience

What do they want? * They want to experience the white water rafting river. They want a convinent method to schedule, book, and plan a white water rafting trip.
What do they except to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website?
* They would be able to find most answers to they questions. For example, What the tour guides offer,
what gear they can rent, pricing, safety concerns, and most imporantantly some photos of the trips to know what to expect.
What are their needs that aren't being met?
* Without the webiste they are unable to see the tours, packages, or schedule as easily
The target audience are those who are wishing to have a thrilling experience on our river. Our rafting company can and will provide that for them.
They are first time riders who want the safty of a guide. Or experience riders who just want to ride every river.
On this website they will be able to find the right tour for them, as well as the proper gear renting packages.
Audiences will be from any device, so we can have all customer schedule and enjoy our river.
